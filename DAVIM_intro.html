Asian Education Group (AEG) is the largest centre for Media Education in Asia with World Class facilities. Mr. Sandeep Marwah, President – Marwah Studios and an International Media Personality who i s heading the organization has been on the National Jury of films earlier also. He has represented India on film jury at Budapest Film Festival – Hungary, Mona Film Festival – Belgium, International Cairo Film Festival – Egypt, Gdansk Film Festival – Poland and many others. Established in November 1991, the Group’s history is a story of innovation and pioneering new technologies. Asian Education Group is the education arm of Marwah Studios Enterprise which has been in media education for past 27 years and has set many International records for which it has been highlighted and been awarded more than 100 times all over World..


International Film and Television Club, International Film and Television Research Centre, International Children’s Film Forum, International Public Broadcasting Forum, Indo Wales Creative Forum, Indo Egypt Cultural Forum, Indo Sudan Cultural Forum and Indo Nigerian Film Association are some of the bodies working hand to hand with AEG. Asian Education Group is also supported by the Film Federation of India, Film Producers Guild, Indian Motion Picture Producers Association, Western India Cinematographers Association, Noida Photographers Association, Broadcasters Club of India and Broadcasting Engineers Society..

The World renowned media centre is also working along with United Nations Information Centre, United Nations Education Scientific and Cultural Organization, PHD Chambers of Commerce, ASSOCHAM, Indo American Chamber of Commerce, World Trade Centre and many more State, National and International agencies. Spread out over two lac square feet area in two campuses in Noida, one in Sector 125 and another in Film City Noida, Asian Education Group is a family of about 10000 media professionals from about 90 countries.

Asian School of Media Studies, Asian School of Media Studies, Asian Business School, Asian School of Communication-UCLAN, Asian School of Graphics and Animation, Asian School of Performing Arts and Asian School of Fashions are jointly conducting 42 different UGC approved Degree and Diploma Media courses (ranging from three months to three years duration). Alumni of Asian Education Group are placed all over the world.
